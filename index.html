How it feels to roll in Genshin Impact

Hey tutors. You might've seen mention of a game called Genshin Impact in the general discord channel this morning. Well, since then, it hasn't left my mind. So me and my mind thought it'd be fun to theme this benchmark around Genshin. Specifically, the 'gacha' system. This system is the japanesse sibling of lootboxes, though the main difference being that this gacha system can be rolled with in-game currency, not just microtransactions. The best characters and weapons have the lowest roll rates. Below, you can find a list of the rarest pulls and a table to showcasing my experience with this system

  1. 5* Characters have a 0.6% chance of pulling
  2. 4* Characters have a 5.1% chance of pulling
  3. 5* Weapons can have either 0.6% or 0.7% chance of pulling depending on the banner
  4. 4* Weapons can have either 5.1% or 6% chance of pulling depending on the banner
  5. 3* Weapons have a 94.3% chance of pulling. There are no 3* characters
  6. Pity system: if a player has not rolled a 4* or above 9 pulls in a row, the 10th is guaranteed to be 4*. Similarly, if a player has not rolled a 5* within 89 pulls, the 90th is quaranteed to be 5*
